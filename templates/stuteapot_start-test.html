Please read the following information.

They concern the use of your data and the goals of the present questionnaire.

Discover Your Learning Styles

Dive into our questionnaire designed to assess your learning styles. By navigating through these questions, you can also contribute to a small research project in the fields of pedagogy and psychology!


Contribute to Pedagogy and Psychology Research

This is not just a survey of your learning profile, but also an opportunity to take part in a research project. By participating, you are helping to better understand the diversity of student's preferences in pedagogy strategies. Your anonymous responses will be invaluable in better understanding the wide variety of student profiles. Remember, your privacy is our priority, and your data will only be used for research purposes and for improving the StuTeaPot website.


Risk and Data Usage

There's absolutely no risk involved in taking part in this study. Your only task will be to answer a series of questions, which generally takes between 20 and 30 minutes. Feel free to pause or leave the questionnaire whenever you like. If you leave the questionnaire before the end, you will not receive the test results. In addition, if you wish to withdraw from the study, even after it has been completed, simply send me an email (email address can be found in the "Find me" section).


Why Your Input is Crucial

Every student is unique, and this uniqueness is reflected in the myriad ways in which learning is processed and teaching strategies are received. The more responses we collect, the more we understand how learning styles and teaching method preferences intersect with personality traits. Your participation is also essential to help other students receive personalized feedback. At the end of the questionnaire, you will be able to describe your favorite study methods and share tips, and those will be shared with other students with profiles similar to yours.
